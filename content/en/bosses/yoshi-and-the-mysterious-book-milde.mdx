<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering Yoshi's Moves for Discovery">
Yoshi retains many of his classic abilities while gaining a new one, the Tail Flick, which is crucial for interacting with the diverse creatures of **Yoshi and the Mysterious Book**. Understanding how to utilize these moves effectively is key to uncovering all the secrets within Mr. E's pages.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Tongue and Egg-cellent Throws">
    Use Yoshi's iconic tongue to gobble up creatures and objects. This can turn them into eggs, which can then be aimed and thrown to interact with other creatures or the environment.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Flutter Jump and Ground Pound">
    These classic Yoshi moves are essential for traversing habitats. The Flutter Jump allows for extended airtime, while the Ground Pound can reveal hidden items or trigger environmental changes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The New Tail Flick">
    The Tail Flick is a new ability that lets Yoshi grab creatures and carry them on his back. This is vital for utilizing a creature's unique abilities, such as making flowers bloom with a Crazee Dayzee.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Creative Creature Interaction">
    Beyond direct moves, creatures themselves offer traversal methods. You might ride them like a surfboard or float within bubbles they create, opening new exploration possibilities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Combat-Free Exploration">
A notable feature in **Yoshi and the Mysterious Book** is that Yoshi cannot be defeated. This allows players to focus entirely on exploration and discovery without the pressure of losing health.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Creature Discovery and Chapter Progression">
The heart of **Yoshi and the Mysterious Book** lies in its unique discovery-based gameplay. Each level is a creature's habitat, initially unnamed, where your goal is to study its traits through various interactions.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Habitat Exploration">
    Each habitat is designed around a specific creature. Interact with it directly or use it to interact with the environment or other creatures to learn its characteristics.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Logging Discoveries">
    After completing the main goal of a habitat, the traits you've learned are written into Mr. E's pages. You'll earn stars for each discovery, with a large discovery awarding three stars and signaling the end of the level.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

Tokens collected during levels can be used to unlock hints for undiscovered traits. Initially, these hints show a silhouette, with more tokens revealing further details. Stars are crucial for unlocking new chapters, which present entirely new environments and creatures to catalog.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Amiibo Integration and Game Details">
**Yoshi and the Mysterious Book** enhances its gameplay with optional amiibo functionality, offering daily rewards and hints to aid your discovery efforts.

Tap any compatible amiibo figure daily to receive a fortune reading from Mr. E. Based on your fortune, you'll earn in-game tokens, which are invaluable for unlocking hints about discoveries you haven't yet made. This provides a consistent incentive to engage with the game and its amiibo features.

<Callout type="warning" title="Amiibo Sold Separately">
Remember that amiibo figures are sold separately. Ensure compatibility by checking amiibo.com for details on functionality before purchasing.
</Callout>
