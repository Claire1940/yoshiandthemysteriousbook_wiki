This design choice allows players to fully immerse themselves in the "creature discovery" aspect without the pressure of difficult boss battles.

The game is divided into 10 chapters, each representing a unique habitat within Mr. E's pages. These chapters include diverse environments such as Wildwoods, Mountaintops, Seaside, Bug Country, and ultimately, The Moon. Progressing through these chapters involves earning stars by making discoveries about the creatures inhabiting each area.

A "large discovery" awards three stars and signals the completion of a level, after which the learned traits are logged in Mr. E's pages. Tokens collected during levels can be used to unlock hints for undiscovered traits, further emphasizing the discovery loop. The culmination of these chapters on "The Moon" suggests a grand finale that is likely thematic and exploratory, rather than a direct confrontation.
