### Reward Types and Values

When you scan an amiibo, you'll primarily receive two types of rewards: coins and tokens. These are crucial for your journey through Mr. E's book.

| Reward Type | Description                                        | Quantity (per scan) | Purpose                                        |
| :---------- | :------------------------------------------------- | :------------------ | :--------------------------------------------- |
| **Coins**   | Standard in-game currency                          | **10 - 200**        | Purchase items, unlock minor secrets           |
| **Tokens**  | Special currency for Mr. E's book discoveries      | **1 - 5**           | Unlock hints, reveal new creatures, progress story |

<Callout type="info" title="Understanding Token Utility">
Tokens are the lifeblood of progression in *Yoshi and the Mysterious Book*. They allow you to unlock "discoveries" or "hints" that are necessary to complete Mr. E's content and fully explore all the quirky creatures within his pages.
</Callout>
