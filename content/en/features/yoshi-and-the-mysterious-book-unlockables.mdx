### Acquiring More Tools

The process of expanding your Exploration Tool collection is straightforward:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete Chapters">
    Finish the first six main chapters of *Yoshi and the Mysterious Book* to initially unlock the Exploration Tools feature.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Collect Smiley Flowers">
    Throughout your adventures, gather as many Smiley Flowers as possible. These are the primary currency for new tools.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Visit Mr. E">
    Access the Exploration Tools menu and locate the "Invent" button. Mr. E will then "think up" a new tool for you.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Purchase Tools">
    Each new tool will cost five Smiley Flowers. Continue collecting and purchasing to expand your arsenal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

There are numerous tools available, so consistent Smiley Flower collection is key to unlocking them all. Only one tool, the All Creature Badge, is unlocked by completing all 10 main chapters, rather than through Smiley Flowers.

</Section>

### Making Discoveries: An Interactive Approach

The game encourages a hands-on approach to learning about its creatures. Yoshi has several "egg-cellent" moves he can use to interact with the environment and its inhabitants:

<CardGrid cols={3}>

    Use Yoshi's long tongue to taste creatures. This can reveal their unique flavors and sometimes turn them into eggs.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Jump & Ground Pound">
    Jumping on creatures or performing a ground pound can trigger different reactions, from popping them to scattering smaller entities.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Egg Toss">
    Throw eggs at objects and creatures. This is a primary method for interaction and often leads to surprising outcomes and Discoveries.
  </Card>

Each habitat contains a "big discovery" that allows you to progress to the next area. However, the true depth lies in uncovering all the smaller, optional Discoveries within each level.

### Amiibo Integration

For players who own amiibo figures, there's a daily bonus to be had. Tapping a compatible amiibo figure to your Nintendo Switch 2 system allows Mr. E to read your fortune. This isn't just for fun; based on the fortune you receive, you'll earn in-game tokens. These tokens can be incredibly useful for unlocking hints about Discoveries you haven't yet made, guiding you towards secrets and completion.

<Callout type="info">
Using amiibo is a great way to get a daily boost of tokens that can help you find elusive Discoveries, making your journey to 100% completion smoother.
</Callout>

### Smiley Flowers: The Universal Currency

Smiley Flowers are not just for show; they are a vital collectible throughout *Yoshi and the Mysterious Book*. As mentioned, they are primarily used to purchase new Exploration Tools from Mr. E. This makes finding every Smiley Flower a significant part of the game's unlockable content.
