<Section icon={Steps} title="Step-by-Step: Completing a Research Page">

To fully document a creature and earn the maximum number of stars, you need to follow a structured approach to exploration. Missing a single trait can prevent you from 100% completing a chapter, which is vital for unlocking late-game content like the HUD customization options.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Observation">
    Enter the habitat and observe the creature's natural behavior. Does it move in patterns? Does it interact with other NPCs like Shy Guys? Log the basic movement trait immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Taste Test">
    Use Yoshi's tongue to "taste" the creature. This is almost always a recorded discovery. Note if the creature can be turned into an egg or if it is too large/bitter to swallow.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Environmental Testing">
    Carry the creature to various elements in the level. Look for "Smiley Flowers" hidden behind objects that only that specific creature can interact with (e.g., heavy boulders that require a drill-nosed creature).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Naming">
    Once the Big Discovery is logged, you will exit the book. You can now name the creature yourself or ask Mr. E for a suggestion. This finalizes the entry in the encyclopedia.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Naming Tip">
Naming a creature isn't just cosmetic; it helps you remember which creature belongs to which ability when you reach the advanced summoning levels in Chapter 6.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Advanced Strategies and Hidden Mechanics">

As you progress toward the latter half of the game, specifically Chapter 6 and beyond, the **yoshi and the mysterious book creature discovery** loop introduces a game-changing mechanic. In certain levels, Yoshi gains the ability to summon any previously discovered creature at will. This recontextualizes the entire game, turning your previous research into a toolkit for solving complex environmental puzzles.

**Expert Research Tips:**
- **Token Management**: Don't hoard your tokens. Spend them on hints for the "Big Discoveries" early on, as these are often tucked away in non-linear sections of the map.
- **Amiibo Support**: Tapping any compatible Amiibo once per day grants a "Fortune" from Mr. E, which rewards you with a significant amount of tokens for hints.
- **Revisiting Habitats**: Some discoveries are impossible to find on your first visit. You may need to return after learning how a specific object (like an explosive nut) works in a later chapter.

| Reward | Token Cost | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Silhouette Hint** | 10 Tokens | Shows the shape of a missing discovery. |
| **Detail Hint** | 25 Tokens | Provides a text clue about the interaction needed. |
| **Location Hint** | 50 Tokens | Marks the general area of the discovery on the map. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Summoning Mastery">
In Chapter 6, use the summoning mechanic to combine creature abilities. For example, summon a bubble-spitter to create a platform, then summon a jetpack creature to reach heights previously inaccessible.
</Callout>
