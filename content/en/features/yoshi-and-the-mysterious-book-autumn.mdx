<Section icon={Swords} title="Autumn Puzzle Solutions: A Step-by-Step Approach">
Navigating the autumn chapters requires a systematic approach to puzzle-solving. Each level, or "chapter," presents a series of challenges that build upon the creatures' abilities. By following a structured method, players can efficiently uncover secrets and complete objectives.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Creature Scan">
    Upon entering a new autumn area, **identify all visible creatures**. Observe their movements and immediate reactions to Yoshi's presence. Mr. E will often provide initial information about their nature.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Experiment with Interactions">
    Systematically try all of Yoshi's basic actions on each creature: **jump, taste, carry, ground pound, and egg toss**. Pay close attention to any unique animations or environmental changes that occur.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize Creature Abilities">
    Once an ability is discovered (e.g., dandelions cracking rocks), look for environmental elements that could benefit from this interaction. For instance, if a rock blocks your path, consider if nearby dandelions can be used.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Combine Abilities for Complex Puzzles">
    Many autumn puzzles require combining the traits of multiple creatures. For example, you might need to carry a flower-like creature to bloom a specific plant, then use a bubble-spouting creature to float up to the newly bloomed platform.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Collect Tokens and Hints">
    If you're stuck, search for **tokens** scattered around the level. These can be exchanged for hints from Mr. E, guiding you towards the next discovery. Scanning compatible amiibo daily can also provide additional tokens.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Seasonal Challenges">
Beware of environmental hazards common in autumn levels, such as falling leaves that obscure paths or strong winds that can alter your jumps. Some creatures might also have defensive reactions unique to the season.
</Callout>
