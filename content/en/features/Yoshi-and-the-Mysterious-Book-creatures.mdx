<Section icon={Map} title="Understanding Creature Discovery Mechanics">

In the vibrant world of *Yoshi and the Mysterious Book*, released May 21, 2026, players are tasked with helping a sentient encyclopedia named Mr. E recover his lost memories. Unlike previous titles, the core gameplay loop revolves entirely around the cataloging of **Yoshi and the Mysterious Book creatures**. Each level represents a specific habitat where creatures reside, and your goal is to experiment with them to uncover hidden traits.

These traits are not just flavor text; they are essential for progression. By interacting with the environment—such as using a Crazee Dayzee to make flowers bloom—you unlock "Discovery Stars." The game features a beautiful paper-illustration art style that highlights interactive elements with painterly textures, making it easier to identify which creatures can be manipulated.

| Discovery Type | Star Reward | Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Minor Discovery** | 1 Star | Interact with a creature in a basic way (e.g., jumping on it) |
| **Environmental Discovery** | 1 Star | Use a creature to trigger a change in the level |
| **Large Discovery** | 3 Stars | Uncover the creature's primary unique trait (Ends the level) |

<Callout type="tip" title="Experimentation is Key">
Since Yoshi cannot be defeated in this game, don't be afraid to take damage or get trapped. Often, getting "hit" by a creature is the only way to log a specific trait in Mr. E's pages.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Abilities and the Tail Flick System">

Yoshi retains his classic moveset, including the Flutter Jump, Ground Pound, and the ability to turn enemies into eggs. However, the introduction of the **Tail Flick** mechanic completely changes how you interact with **Yoshi and the Mysterious Book creatures**. By flicking his tail, Yoshi can grab a creature and carry it on his back. While carrying a creature, Yoshi gains access to its innate powers.

For example, carrying a Plumebrella allows Yoshi to glide safely over large gaps, while carrying a Swirm allows you to create slime platforms. This synergy is required to reach high-altitude secrets and hidden tokens.

<Callout type="warning" title="Hazardous Interactions">
Not all interactions are helpful. The **Glubbit** in the Seaside levels creates large bubbles that can trap Yoshi. While this might seem like a hindrance, being trapped in a bubble is sometimes the only way to reach floating platforms in the sky.
</Callout>

### Specialized Creatures and Objects
Beyond the standard enemies, some creatures act more like vehicles or tools. The **Small Snurfboard** is a skateboard-like creature that Yoshi can ride to gain massive speed, which is essential for certain "Discovery" challenges that require hitting multiple triggers in a short window. Additionally, **Boulders** often block your path, but they can be weakened if you find a dandelion growing on them, allowing Yoshi to destroy them with a Ground Pound.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Complete Your Field Guide">

Completing Mr. E's field guide requires more than just finishing the levels. You must find every hidden trait for all **Yoshi and the Mysterious Book creatures**. If you are struggling to find a specific discovery, the game provides a hint system fueled by tokens.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect Tokens">
    Gather tokens hidden throughout each habitat or earn them by tapping compatible Amiibo figures once per day.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock Silhouettes">
    Spend tokens at Mr. E's menu to reveal a silhouette of the missing discovery. This gives you a visual clue of what creature or object is involved.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Purchase Details">
    If the silhouette isn't enough, spend additional tokens to get a text description of the interaction needed (e.g., "What happens when it inhales something?").
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute and Log">
    Return to the habitat and perform the action. Once the star appears, the trait is permanently logged in the book.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Amiibo Functionality">
Tapping an Amiibo figure allows Mr. E to read your fortune. Depending on the result, you can receive a significant boost in tokens, making it much easier to complete the later chapters like "The Moon."
</Callout>

</Section>
