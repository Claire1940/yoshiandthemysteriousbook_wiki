<Section icon={Target} title="Gameplay Overview and Key Features">
Yoshi and the Mysterious Book invites players to step into a living encyclopedia named Mr. E. As Yoshi, your primary goal is to help Mr. E recall the diverse creatures residing within his pages. This involves exploring colorful habitats, experimenting with each creature, and uncovering surprising facts. The game emphasizes interaction and discovery, encouraging players to engage with the environment in creative ways.

One of the standout features is the ability to interact with creatures to solve puzzles and traverse levels. Yoshi can gobble up creatures, turn them into eggs, or even use them to blow bubbles, each action leading to different outcomes and discoveries.

Here's a quick look at some core gameplay elements:

These chapters are teeming with unique creatures, environments, and secrets waiting to be found. The game challenges players to fully explore each area, using Yoshi's abilities and the creatures' traits to progress.

Let's break down the typical progression through a chapter:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enter a Chapter">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Interact with Creatures">
    Encounter creatures like the Crazee Dayzee. Experiment with them using Yoshi's moves (gobble, jump, carry) to learn their characteristics and abilities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Make Discoveries">
    Each interaction can lead to a "discovery," revealing new information or triggering events. Some discoveries can even help you learn about creatures from previous chapters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Uncover Big Discoveries">
    Every habitat has a major "big discovery" to uncover. Finding this allows you to advance to the next area, though you can always return for more exploration.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Utilize Amiibo (Optional)">

  </Step>
</Steps>
