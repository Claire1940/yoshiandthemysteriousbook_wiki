<Section icon={Swords} title="Gameplay Mechanics and Creature Interactions">
**Yoshi and the Mysterious Book** introduces innovative gameplay centered around interacting with the inhabitants of Mr. E's pages. Yoshi will use his unique abilities to understand and utilize these creatures to solve environmental puzzles and advance through levels. Each creature possesses distinct traits that can be leveraged in creative ways.

### Yoshi's Action Capabilities

| Action        | Description                                     | Effect on Creatures/Environment                                     |
| :------------ | :---------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Jump**      | Standard jump, can be used on creatures.        | Triggers reactions, activates platforms, or reveals items.           |
| **Taste/Eat** | Yoshi uses his tongue to taste or gobble.       | Provides clues, grants temporary abilities, or produces eggs.       |
| **Carry**     | Yoshi carries certain creatures on his back.    | Combines creature abilities with Yoshi's movement, opens new paths. |
| **Ground Pound**| Mid-air ground pound.                           | Shakes loose items, breaks certain objects, or impacts creatures.   |
