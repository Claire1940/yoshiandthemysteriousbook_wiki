*Yoshi and the Mysterious Book* is the latest entry in the beloved puzzle-platformer series, known for its charming aesthetics and accessible gameplay. This installment continues the tradition with hand-drawn storybook-style graphics and levels brimming with unique creatures.

The game's narrative centers around Mr. E, a talking encyclopedia that falls from the sky onto the Yoshis' island. This reference book is not just for reading; its pages contain entries about strange creatures and portals leading to their worlds. Yoshi's adventure involves entering these worlds to learn about the creatures, adding discoveries to the encyclopedia.
