### Amiibo Interaction Guide

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Tap Your Amiibo">
    Locate the amiibo touchpoint on your Nintendo Switch 2 console or controller. Gently tap your **Collector's Edition Yoshi Amiibo** figure to the touchpoint.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Receive Your Fortune">
    Mr. E will read your fortune, offering insights or humorous predictions for your day's adventure. This interaction happens daily.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Earn In-Game Tokens">
    Based on the fortune received, you will be awarded in-game tokens. These tokens are crucial for unlocking hints about discoveries you might have missed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock Hints & Discoveries">
    Use the accumulated tokens to purchase hints that guide you towards uncovering challenging discoveries within Mr. E's pages. This can reveal new creature reactions or hidden secrets.
  </Step>
</Steps>
