Initial previews and hands-on experiences suggest that *Yoshi and the Mysterious Book* offers a surprisingly deep and engaging platforming adventure. Unlike previous Yoshi titles like *Woolly World* and *Crafted World*, this new installment aims to strike a balance between being accessible for kids and challenging for seasoned Yoshi fans.

The game's core mechanic revolves around Yoshi diving into a mysterious book named Mr. E, where he explores various lands and learns about unique creatures. - **Yoshi's Moves:** Standard moves like gobbling enemies and throwing eggs, plus a new tail flick.
