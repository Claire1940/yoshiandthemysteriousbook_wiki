<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Progress: The Discovery System">

Unlike traditional "left-to-right" platformers, the **Yoshi and the Mysterious Book platforms** experience is built around experimentation. You are dropped into habitats and tasked with learning everything about the local fauna. This "biology textbook" approach requires a specific set of steps to master.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Interact with Everything">
    Lick, lift, and lob every creature you see. Each action can trigger a new discovery entry in your log.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify the Big Discovery">
    Every habitat has one "Big Discovery" required to unlock the next area. This usually involves a complex interaction with a major creature.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Collect Smiley Flowers">
    Find 3-6 flowers per level to unlock post-game UI elements. These are often hidden behind environmental puzzles.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Spend Tokens for Hints">
    If you are stuck on a specific discovery, use the tokens earned from Amiibo or gameplay to reveal the missing requirements.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The variety of creatures is the game's greatest strength. From the **Crazee Dayzee** that appears in multiple colors to the **Glubbit** that traps Yoshi in bubbles, every encounter is a mini-puzzle. You even have the freedom to name every creature you discover, creating a personalized research journal.

<Callout type="success" title="Advanced Mechanic">
In Chapter 6, you unlock the ability to **summon any previously met creature**. This is essential for solving late-game puzzles that require multi-creature interactions.
</Callout>
