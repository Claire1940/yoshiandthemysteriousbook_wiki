<Section icon={Shield} title="Optimizing Your Nintendo Switch 2 Storage">
Managing your Nintendo Switch 2 storage effectively will ensure you always have room for your favorite games and future releases. With "Yoshi and the Mysterious Book" clocking in at 19.6 GB, a few simple steps can help you stay organized.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Check Current Storage">
    Navigate to System Settings > Data Management to see how much free space is available on your console and microSD card. This gives you a clear picture of your current storage situation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Archive Unused Software">
    Instead of deleting games, archive them. This removes the game data but keeps the icon on your Home menu and, more importantly, preserves your save data. You can re-download the game anytime. Go to System Settings > Data Management > Manage Software.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Delete Unnecessary Data">
    If you have screenshots, videos, or game save data from games you'll never play again, you can delete them. Be cautious with save data, as it cannot be recovered once deleted unless backed up to the cloud.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Consider a Larger MicroSD Card">
    If archiving isn't enough, upgrading your microSD card is the best long-term solution. The Nintendo Switch 2 supports various capacities, allowing you to store dozens of games.
  </Step>
</Steps>
