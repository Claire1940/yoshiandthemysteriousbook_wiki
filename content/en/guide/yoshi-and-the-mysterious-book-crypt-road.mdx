### How to Maximize Discoveries

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Experiment with Yoshi's Moves">
    Yoshi has a versatile set of moves, including gobbling creatures with his tongue, jumping on them, or even turning them into eggs. Each creature reacts differently to these actions, leading to new discoveries. For example, some creatures might spit out a Smiley Flower when they inhale a Crazee Dayzee.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Observe Creature Behavior">
    Pay close attention to how creatures move, sound, and react to their environment. Their behavior often hints at what kind of interaction will yield a discovery. Some creatures might pop when jumped on, while others might blow bubbles.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Revisit Habitats with New Knowledge">
    As you progress, you'll gain insights that can help you find discoveries in earlier chapters. The game explicitly states that "Some discoveries can help you learn about other creatures, including things from previous chapters you might have missed."
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Amiibo for Hints">
    Tap a compatible amiibo figure daily to get your fortune read by Mr. E. These fortunes provide in-game tokens that can be used to unlock hints about discoveries you haven't made yet. This can be a game-changer for those elusive finds.
  </Step>
</Steps>
