### The Exploration Process

The core gameplay loop involves exploring pages, identifying wiggling objects or elements that catch your eye, and using a magnifying glass to click on them. This process leads to discoveries, which are then added to the book's pages.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify Interactive Elements">
    Look for wiggling objects or sparkling spots on the page. These indicate points of interest for discovery. Use your magnifying glass to zoom in.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Engage with Discoveries">
    Click on the identified elements. This might involve solving a small puzzle, interacting with a creature, or simply observing a new phenomenon. For example, blooming three big flowers to complete an objective.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Name and Document Creatures">
    Upon discovery, you'll often have the opportunity to name new creatures. This personalizes your journey and fills the pages of the mysterious book. The game suggests names like "Crazy Daisy" or "Bunchabee" which you can accept or customize.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Collect and Complete">
    Gather items like Smiley Flowers or honey during exploration. Completing objectives and collecting all items in an area allows you to "end exploration" and progress.
  </Step>
</Steps>
