Advancing through *Yoshi and the Mysterious Book* requires a different mindset than traditional platformers. You are not just running to the end of a level; you are studying an ecosystem. To unlock the next chapter, you must satisfy Mr. E's curiosity.

  <Step num="2" title="Interact with Creatures">
